# Understanding Shishi and Fuchs
> Pinterest boards:
[Fuchs](https://www.pinterest.com/bjzeit/f%C3%BCxta/)
 | [Shishi](https://www.pinterest.com/bjzeit/shishi/)

**Overview:** Shishi (she-she) is “red”, Fuchs (fyookz) is “blue”. They used to be at war, but are now making a more concerted effort to understand one another and get along.

## Shishi
> Alter egos: Dionysian, Id, Right Brain, Oasis


Shishi is animalic, and vaguely psychotic. She’s also warm, empathetic, a deep feeler, free-associative, highly creative, rebellious, childish, vital. She’s horizontal, affective, aesthetic, hateful, joyful, ecstatic. She’s not purely emotion/emotionality, because she also can be very watchful and observant. She doesn’t always make herself known. She enjoys fantasy and can be very dissociative. She’s driven by senses and has a strong physicality. 

She is a loner and struggles to integrate socially. She also struggles to leave her own imagination/ideas, leading to a sense of her inferiorityin contrast to Fuchs. She calls this a “compentency wound” or sometimes an “efficacy complex”. She loves vocabulary, memes, horses, thunderstorms, dancing, singing/belting, smelling, soft fabric, animal bones, fur, alcohol, psilocybin mushrooms, Rothko, anime, surrealism/symbolism, painting, Lana del Rey,Adele, and musical theater. She is prone to having visions and speaking in tongues. She’s seeking, constantly dissatisfied, and immensely self destructive. Her sensory sensitivity is always vivid, and it is a place in which she can live. Her mental images/constructions are so real as to be borderline illusory, as though they are more real than reality. This makes it difficult to communicate with other people, and leads to a profound shame/self hatred. Despite her connection to the imaginary, she is intensely embodied and connected to her senses. She is obsessive, and has an idiosyncratic but vivid way of describing/expressing her experiences, desires, dreams, needs, and fears.

She is very passionate about other people, but tends to prefer her own images of them to their/her actual experience. This can lead to a sense of distrust and alienation. However, she is able to revel in this alienation, so sometimes she is blind to taking interpersonal problems seriously  until they erupt into a crisis. Shishi is capable of oceanic feeling, religiosity, and can be somewhat devouring (despite also being shivering and submissive). She is romantic, passionate, delusional, funny, tragic. 

Shishi is decisively **female**.
### Artistry
Shishi has visions, hallucinations, and can create images out of nothing. She’s meandering, divergent, contradictory, and surprising. 

She’s accepting, willing to take anything at its face, and willing to forego pre-conceptions or pre-fab ideologies. She’s willing to be uncomfortable while doing something new, as long as Fuchs holds the discomfort for her. When she has to hold her own discomfort, she panics and cries, because it is so painful and disorienting. However, when Fuchs is more fully activated, Shishi can be completely open. 

She thrives on tracing, copying, doodling, and has deep insight into nonverbal visual/aesthetic/sensory cues. E.g., she can discern between the quality of two near identical brands of paper, whether the weight/stroke/texture of a drawn line is adequate, whether the tonality of a color palette is harmonious or discordant, whether a scent is too metallic, whether lights are too bright. She is able to communicate emotions vividly and express primal, archetypal experiences.

## Fuchs
> Alter egos: Apollonian, Superego, Left Brain, Blur

Fuchs is self hating, critical, and serious. She’s also logical, an animus, linear, masculine, vital. She likes diagrams, lists, data, information, systems, structures, architecture, geometry, composition, hierarchy, direction, classical/experimental/ambient music, Renaissance art, technology/creative code, silhouettes, and riding on airplanes. She’s also resentful, bitter, superior, bipolar, black-and-white, and very fearful. 

She insists on opposing Shishi’s desires: you want to draw? you should learn to code instead. You like english class? You should like math instead. She’s fearful that Shishi will fail, embarass herself, or alienate both of them. She’s simultaneously afraid and protective of Shishi: she knows that Shishi is more “real”, by being more attached to Betsy’s immediate experience through emotion and sense. By being a function of willpower, Fuchs is more distant from the core self. If she fails to restrain Shishi, Shishi will not survive the attacks of the outside world.

 There is an emotionality to Fuchs as well: she’s insecure, ambitious, and has extremely refined taste. She is satisfied when she can coexist with Shishi but stills hates that Shishi get to perpetually/indelibly be at the center; she grieves that Shishi is the lifeforce in a world where, materially, Betsy would be better served by having Fuchs as the lifeforce and Shishi as decoration. She feels immense pain at the effort it takes for her to exist, but knows that if she stopped existing, Shishi would slip the scales into chaos. She doesn’t want Shishi to embarass herself by showing up to a masquerade with her own face, thus, Fuchs is prone to acting Machiavellian.
 
Because she’s so much more conceptual than corporeal, you could describe Fuchs as **female, male, androgynous, or mechanical**, and you would be equally right every time.

### Artistry
She resents being advised that Betsy’s art is better served by Shishi, because she feels she has made a vital contribution to her aesthetic development. Fuchs is Apollonian: aka, beauty from order, grace, and reason. Fuchs compels Betsy to read poetry, annotate books, compose images, and articulate herself. She can sometimes over exert herself, trying to manipulate every application. 

## Mutual dependency
Shishi needs Fuchs to enforce correct behavior, financial literacy, regularity, task-accomplishment, socially positive behavior, emotional regulation. Fuchs needs Shishi in order to survive and experience self-love— actually to experience life at all.

You would think that Shishi is more empathetic, but its Fuchs who needs to enforce empathy at times, because Shishi is so avoidant of shame that she would rather live in her own construction than read between the lines. She also needs Fuchs to define/articulate/enforce plans for resolving and enduring breaks in connection, which she finds unbearable. Like, Shishi needed to be taught the word “connection” or “break in connection”— she would’ve just said “[Person] hates me/they never loved me at all” or “[Person] loves me better than I ever could love them, I’m ungrateful and awful” in rapid succession until she attempts suicide. Fuchs is her protector, her prince, her saving grace. 

## Attitudes
Shishi is prone to forgetting that Fuchs is actually a part of her: she is always placing Fuchs outside herself. 

Fuchs resents not being totally integrated into the self, but is also aware that by trying too much to embed herself in the core-self, she can damage or hurt Shishi’s vulnerable self esteem. Shishi fears Fuchs doesn’t love her romantically, the way Shishi loves Fuchs. 

Fuchs fears that Shishi will expel her or destroy her given half a chance, so there’s some truth to this. Sometimes, Fuchs imagines she will leave entirely, and Shishi will take over. 

Both sides are very aware of the precarity of Fuchs’ presence, and dread a rupture despite repeated attempts to force a break/split.
